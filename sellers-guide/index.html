Before Your Home Is Shown

Position your home as the most desirable property in its price range. These preparations will set it apart from the competition.

  • Declutter, clean, and organize
  • Maintain the exterior
  • Fresh paint where needed
  • Make necessary repairs
  • Brighten your home
  • Remove pets during showings
  • Hire a pre-listing home inspector

Staging Tips

Staging helps present your home in the best possible light and appeals to the widest audience.

  • Eliminate excess or oversized furniture
  • Remove clutter from all surfaces
  • Maximize natural light in every room
  • Minimize personal photos and items
  • Add plants or flowers to outdoor spaces
  • Use neutral colors and décor
  • Emphasize each room's functionality
