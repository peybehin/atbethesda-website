Overview

Planned Luxury on the Bethesda/Potomac Edge

Avenel is a planned luxury community on the border of Bethesda and Potomac, Maryland, associated with TPC Potomac at Avenel Farm — the venue that hosted the PGA Tour’s Kemper Open for years. It offers a mix of large single-family homes and townhomes in a planned-community setting with a different character than Bethesda’s older, more organic neighborhoods.

The community feel, amenity associations, and golf-course adjacency attract buyers who value those lifestyle elements.

Start with the Bethesda neighborhood guide or the Potomac guide for the wider market, or read on for everything specific to Avenel.

Location

Where Is Avenel?

Avenel is on the Bethesda/Potomac border, generally near River Road and the I-495/I-270 corridor. TPC Potomac at Avenel Farm is the defining landmark.

Access to Potomac Village and downtown Bethesda is by car. Avenel typically uses Potomac/20854 ZIP codes, so confirm the specific property’s ZIP.

Boundaries and community sections vary; confirm a specific property before relying on it.

Quick Answers

Avenel FAQ

Is Avenel in Bethesda or Potomac? +

Avenel sits on the border of Bethesda and Potomac, Maryland, and typically uses Potomac / 20854 ZIP codes. Confirm the specific property’s ZIP before relying on school or commute assumptions.

Is Avenel a golf-course community? +

Yes. Avenel is associated with TPC Potomac at Avenel Farm, a course that hosted the PGA Tour’s Kemper Open for years, and some homes are positioned near or overlooking it.

What types of homes are in Avenel? +

Avenel offers a mix of large luxury single-family homes and townhomes in a planned-community setting, including golf-adjacent properties and estate-style homes.

Are there townhomes in Avenel? +

Yes. In addition to single-family homes, Avenel includes townhomes and villas for buyers wanting a lower-maintenance luxury lifestyle.

What should buyers know about HOA fees in Avenel? +

As a planned community, Avenel carries HOA / community fees and may have deed restrictions or covenants. Understand these costs and rules before bidding.

What schools serve Avenel? +

Avenel is served by Montgomery County Public Schools, but assignments vary by address — often within Potomac service areas — and change over time. Always confirm a specific home’s assignment directly with MCPS.

How often do homes sell in Avenel? +

Rarely — only 5 homes sold in 2025 (Bright MLS). The market is very thin, so each transaction carries outsized weight in the averages.
