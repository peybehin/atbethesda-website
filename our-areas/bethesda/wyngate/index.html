Overview

Established and Inside the Beltway

Wyngate is an established Bethesda neighborhood inside the Capital Beltway, known for residential streets lined with older homes — many of which have been renovated, expanded, or fully rebuilt. Its location near Old Georgetown Road, Wildwood Shopping Center, and easy access to I-270, I-495, and downtown Bethesda makes it popular with buyers who want an inside-the-Beltway address.

In 2025, Wyngate homes sold at 100.4% of original list price with an average of just 12 days on market — a fast, competitive market.

Housing

Homes in Wyngate

Wyngate homes are predominantly older single-family houses — Colonials, Cape Cods, ramblers, and split-levels — many of which have been renovated, expanded, or fully rebuilt. Original condition vs. fully rebuilt can mean dramatic differences in price and livability.

In 2025, 18 homes sold at a median price of about $1,091,250, averaging roughly $1.24M and selling at 100.4% of original list price with an average of just 12 days on market (Bright MLS). All recorded sales were detached homes.

Quick Answers

Wyngate FAQ

Is Wyngate inside the Beltway? +

Yes. Wyngate sits inside the Capital Beltway (I-495) in Bethesda, in ZIP code 20814, which is a large part of its commuting appeal.

What types of homes are in Wyngate? +

Wyngate is mostly older single-family homes — Colonials, Cape Cods, ramblers, and split-levels — many of which have been renovated, expanded, or fully rebuilt.

Is Wyngate close to Wildwood Shopping Center? +

Yes. Wildwood Shopping Center is a defining nearby landmark and handles everyday shopping just minutes from the neighborhood.

How does Wyngate compare to Alta Vista? +

Both are established inside-the-Beltway Bethesda neighborhoods with similar housing stock. Wyngate’s 2025 median was about $1.09M, and homes there moved very fast, averaging just 12 days on market.

What schools serve Wyngate? +

Wyngate is served by Montgomery County Public Schools, but assignments vary by address and change over time. Always confirm a specific home’s assignment directly with MCPS.

How fast do homes sell in Wyngate? +

Very fast. In 2025, homes averaged just 12 days on market and sold at about 100.4% of original list price (Bright MLS).

What should buyers know? +

Wyngate is competitive, so be pre-approved and ready before you tour. Understand the difference between original-condition and fully rebuilt homes, which can mean dramatic differences in price and livability.
