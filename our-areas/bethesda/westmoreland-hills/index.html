Overview

Close to DC, Fully in Bethesda

Westmoreland Hills sits on the Maryland side of the DC border, just across from Friendship Heights and Tenleytown. Its address gives buyers something rare: a fully suburban setting — large lots, mature trees, traditional homes — within a very short commute to Northwest DC.

Massachusetts Avenue and Western Avenue are the main arteries. In 2025, the median sold price was $1.76M with 19 homes sold and an average of just 17 days on market.

Commute

Commuting From Westmoreland Hills

Westmoreland Hills offers one of the shortest commutes into Northwest DC of any suburban Bethesda neighborhood. Massachusetts Avenue and Western Avenue lead directly into the city.

Friendship Heights and Tenleytown — both with Metro stations on the Red Line — are close by, offering a quick rail option into downtown DC.

The Capital Beltway (I-495) is accessible nearby for regional travel.

Quick Answers

Westmoreland Hills FAQ

Is Westmoreland Hills in Bethesda or Chevy Chase? +

Westmoreland Hills is in southern Bethesda/Chevy Chase, Maryland, generally near Massachusetts Avenue, Western Avenue, and Westmoreland Circle, in ZIP code 20816. It carries a Bethesda mailing identity while sitting close to Chevy Chase and the DC line.

How close is it to Washington, DC? +

Very close. Westmoreland Hills sits on the Maryland side of the DC border, just across from Friendship Heights and Tenleytown, with a short commute into Northwest DC via Massachusetts Avenue.

What types of homes are in Westmoreland Hills? +

The neighborhood is known for traditional luxury homes — Colonials, Tudors, and brick traditionals — on generous, mature lots, with some newer custom builds.

Is Westmoreland Hills walkable to Metro? +

Friendship Heights and Tenleytown Metro stations (Red Line) are nearby, though walkability depends on the specific address. Many residents drive or walk a short distance to reach the stations.

What schools serve Westmoreland Hills? +

Westmoreland Hills is served by Montgomery County Public Schools, but assignments vary by address and change over time. Always confirm a specific home’s assignment directly with MCPS.

How often do homes sell? +

Demand is steady — 19 homes sold in 2025, moving quickly at an average of 17 days on market and selling at about 98.5% of list price (Bright MLS).

What should buyers know? +

Homes move fast here, so be pre-approved and decisive. Lot, condition, and architecture drive significant pricing differences within the neighborhood.
