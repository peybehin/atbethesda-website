Overview

Western Bethesda’s Family Anchor

Wood Acres — listed on Bright MLS as “Woodacres” — is one of western Bethesda’s most sought-after residential neighborhoods, driven by a combination of school identity, location, and established neighborhood character.

Homes here sell quickly: in 2025, the average days on market was just 13 and homes sold at 99.6% of list price, with 22 transactions totaling nearly $30M. The neighborhood sits near Massachusetts Avenue and within reach of MacArthur Boulevard, Glen Echo, and Bannockburn.

Location

Where Is Wood Acres?

Wood Acres is in western Bethesda, generally near Massachusetts Avenue, with access to MacArthur Boulevard. It borders Glen Echo Heights, Bannockburn, and Kenwood.

DC access is via Massachusetts Avenue. (Note: the MLS spelling is “Woodacres” as one word, though the neighborhood is commonly written “Wood Acres.”)

Housing

Homes in Wood Acres

Wood Acres homes are established single-family houses — Colonials, Cape Cods, ramblers, and split-levels — many expanded, renovated, or custom-built, well suited to families.

In 2025, 22 homes sold at a median price of about $1,350,875, averaging roughly $1.35M and selling at 99.6% of original list price with an average of just 13 days on market — nearly $30M in total volume (Bright MLS). All recorded sales were detached homes.

Quick Answers

Wood Acres FAQ

What schools serve Wood Acres? +

Wood Acres is served by Montgomery County Public Schools and is closely associated with Wood Acres Elementary School, but assignments vary by address and change over time. Always confirm a specific home’s assignment directly with MCPS.

Is Wood Acres close to DC? +

Yes. Wood Acres is in western Bethesda near Massachusetts Avenue, which offers a direct route into Northwest DC.

What types of homes are in Wood Acres? +

Wood Acres is mostly established single-family homes — Colonials, Cape Cods, ramblers, and split-levels — many expanded, renovated, or custom-built.

How does Wood Acres compare to Glen Echo Heights and Bannockburn? +

All three are western Bethesda neighborhoods. Wood Acres is more conventionally suburban and family-oriented with a strong school identity, while Glen Echo Heights and Bannockburn are more wooded and architecturally varied.

How fast do homes sell in Wood Acres? +

Very fast. In 2025, homes averaged just 13 days on market and sold at about 99.6% of original list price across 22 transactions (Bright MLS).

Is it spelled “Woodacres” or “Wood Acres”? +

Both appear. The neighborhood is commonly written “Wood Acres” (two words), but Bright MLS lists it as “Woodacres” (one word). They refer to the same neighborhood.

What should buyers know? +

Wood Acres moves quickly with near-list pricing, so get financing ready and be prepared to act. Confirm the specific school assignment directly with MCPS rather than relying on the neighborhood name alone.
