About the Area

Why People Love Potomac

Potomac is synonymous with prestige. Grand estates set on expansive lots, equestrian properties, and a sense of quiet seclusion that's increasingly rare this close to a major city. If privacy and space are your priorities, Potomac delivers both in abundance.

The housing stock here is among the most diverse in the region — from stately brick colonials with sweeping lawns to contemporary architectural masterpieces overlooking the Potomac River. Many homes sit on one acre or more, with mature trees, pools, and guest houses.

While Potomac is car-dependent, it's well worth it for buyers who value space, elite schools, and a prestige address. Churchill and Wootton high schools are perennially ranked among Maryland's best, and private school options abound nearby.
