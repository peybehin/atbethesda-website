About the Area

Living in Chevy Chase DC

Chevy Chase DC — the portion of Chevy Chase that falls within Washington, DC's borders — is among the most serene and prestigious residential neighborhoods in the entire city. Tree-canopied streets, substantial single-family homes, and a neighborhood scale that feels more like Bethesda than downtown DC make it perennially popular with ambassadors, senior government officials, and DC old money. Red Line Metro access at Friendship Heights keeps it connected without sacrificing the quiet.

Why Chevy Chase DC?

  • ✓ Red Line Metro at Friendship Heights
  • ✓ Substantial single-family homes
  • ✓ DC address, suburban feel
  • ✓ Chevy Chase Club & Recreation Assoc.
  • ✓ Connecticut Avenue corridor
